# Lead Ranking Tool — Roadmap

> These are Claude Code hours — time working with AI assistance, not traditional development hours. A developer working alone would multiply these by 3-5x.

## Total estimated: 187 Claude Code hours

## v1 — Ship it

### HubSpot CRM Integration (~12 hours)
Build real-time lead import system with automatic sync, contact deduplication logic, and webhook handling for CRM updates.

### Multi-Channel Scoring Engine (~15 hours)
Develop lead scoring system that weighs email opens, social media interactions, and form submissions with configurable scoring rules interface.

### Lead Ranking Dashboard (~10 hours)
Create dashboard displaying conversion probability scores with source channel filtering and sortable lead lists.

### Automated Follow-up System (~13 hours)
Implement configurable score thresholds that trigger personalized email sequences with execution tracking and token-based personalization.

### Cross-Platform Engagement Tracking (~18 hours)
Aggregate interaction data from Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and Google Ads into unified lead profiles with engagement timeline.

### AI-Powered Lead Analysis (~8 hours)
Integrate ChatGPT/Claude to analyze lead communication patterns, detect intent signals, and generate automated quality predictions.

## Roadmap — Planned

### Advanced Lead Qualification Filtering (~8 hours)
Build custom criteria builder for filtering leads by source, behavior patterns, and demographic data with saved filter presets.

### Automated Performance Reports (~6 hours)
Generate daily and weekly reports with conversion metrics, channel ROI analysis, and score distribution insights with email delivery.

## Idea Board — Exploring

### SMS Lead Nurturing Sequences (~10 hours)
Integrate Twilio for score-based SMS campaigns with timing optimization and content personalization based on lead behavior.

### Predictive Lead Lifecycle Modeling (~15 hours)
Develop machine learning models that estimate optimal contact timing and channel preference based on historical conversion data.

### Lead Velocity Scoring (~12 hours)
Implement time-based scoring that tracks how quickly leads progress through the funnel and identifies acceleration opportunities.

### Competitive Intelligence Integration (~14 hours)
Connect with tools like SEMrush or Ahrefs to factor competitor analysis and market positioning into lead scoring algorithms.

### Social Listening Integration (~16 hours)
Monitor social media mentions and conversations related to leads' companies to enhance scoring with market sentiment data.

### Lead Handoff Automation (~9 hours)
Automate lead assignment to sales team members based on territory, expertise, workload, and lead characteristics.

### Conversation Intelligence (~20 hours)
Integrate with call recording platforms to analyze sales conversations and update lead scores based on conversation outcomes and sentiment.
